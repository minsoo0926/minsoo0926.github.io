## Humor and Taste
> Apr 14, 2026, by Minsoo Ha

&ensp;When the life goes hard, we usually firstly give up laughter. However, anytime you feel life is tough and everything is going wrong, you might think laughter is not direct solution of your problem, but sometimes (almost always), laughter can be the solution of **your life** itself. I want to share my ideas about **humor and taste**, which is one of the most important things in my life, and I hope it can be helpful for you to find happiness and self-fulfillment in your life.

&ensp;**Taste** is one essential ingredient of happiness. It is not just about food, but also about everything we like and dislike in our daily life. It can be about music, art, fashion, or even people. There is no good or bad taste, but it is important to find your own taste and enjoy it. I love rap and rock music, especially <em>Kanye West</em>'s music (my best album is <em>\<Ye\> </em>btw), and recently I listen to a lot of 80s soul music. I like funny and violent movies, and I also like to watch <em>Tarantino</em> and <em>Qubrick</em> movies. I like bouldering and running empty circuit when every light is off. And I like people sharing their favorites with me. These make me feel alive, happy, and identify me.

&ensp;**Humor** is what makes your life more enjoyable. Meeting friends and loving people, sharing jokes and silly stories, and laughing together can keep you from frustration and stress. Everytime tough situation comes, I try to laugh out loud and find something funny in it. After then, some solution can be found, and when everything finishes, I share the story with my friends with some cigarettes and laughter. Being frustrated and doing nothing pushes you in vicious circle, the only way to get out of it is finding some tiny and trivial but funny things in it and enjoy it.

&ensp;When taste meets humor, it can be the best combination for your life. It can make you more creative and open-minded, and sometimes become shelter for you when you are in hard times. So try to find your own taste and humor, share with your friends, and enjoy your life. It will be the best way to find happiness and self-fulfillment in your life.
